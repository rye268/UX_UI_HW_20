OVERVIEW

The Leacock Foundation is a non-profit organization that provides literacy and leadership opportunities to underserved communities. Our goal was to revamp their webite (desktop & mobile) in a way that would increase user engagement, improve on usability and ultimately enable Leacock to attract more donations and volunteers to their incredible cause.

SOLUTION

Make it easy to get involved

Donations, volunteering, fundraising and any other type of information should be clearly outlined and easy to implement on the site.

Users should see how they make an impact

Use of community stories, struggles and updates on programs is key to building a vibrant, involved community

Users should feel secure

Transparency on financials and operations that is easy to understand

First, we needed to hone in on user motivations & pain points
